Products & Services

With over 40-years’ experience in swimming pool surfacing and renovation, we provide specialized pool products and resurfacing solutions for everything from family pools to large commercial, community, and competition swimming pools and other aquatic facilities. We are committed to providing only the highest quality workmanship, materials and products.

Upon request, we can assess your pool to ensure it meets national pool and safety standards and make recommendations to meet ADA requirements.

  • Pool Interiors
  • New Coping, Tile, and Deck Topping
  • Specialty Projects
  • Complete Renovations

Despite what you may have been told, the vast majority of old pools – even 1930’s WPA pools with serious structure cracks – can be made new again with our modern products and methods. We’ve restored every type of outdated pool for a fraction of what it would cost to build a new pool, demonstrating our expertise with concrete, aluminum, steel, acrylic panel pools, as well as one-piece fiberglass pools and spas.
